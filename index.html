Uruguay XXI brought a 45-member delegation to South Summit Brazil, leads the Uruguayan technology pavilion at eMerge Americas this week, and coordinates a 500-company exporter catalog alongside CUTI and ANII. Behind that sits a Competitive Intelligence function with a published sectoral cadence, the Uruguay country-brand architecture covering Technology, Wine, Wools, Meats and Audiovisual, and outcome reporting that climbs to a board chaired by the Minister of Foreign Affairs with five other ministers and the OPP director.

Esa combinación es trabajo de coordinación a escala nacional. La mayoría de las agencias lo resolverían con más personal. Uruguay XXI claramente eligió otro camino. That combination is coordination work at national scale. Most agencies would solve it with more headcount. Uruguay XXI has clearly chosen a different path. Matt from Eduba's team spent time on the Spanish and English sites, walked the technology pavilion notes, read the March 2025 administration change and the 2025-2029 National Budget releases, and shaped the hypothesis on this page.

02

60, 30, 10. Dónde debería vivir cada pieza. 60, 30, 10. Where each piece should live.

La mayoría de las instituciones dirige el esfuerzo de IA a la capa equivocada. Nuestra lectura del ciclo de una delegación o de un reporte sectorial se ordena así. Alrededor del 60% del trabajo es código tradicional y base de datos. Cerca del 30% es lógica basada en reglas, el tipo de si-esto-entonces-aquello que no debería tocar un modelo de lenguaje. Aproximadamente el 10% es un problema de IA genuino, y casi siempre es más acotado de lo que parece.

Most institutions direct their AI effort to the wrong layer. Our read of a delegation cycle or a sectoral reporting cycle sorts out this way. Roughly 60% of the work is traditional code and database. About 30% is rule-based logic, the kind of if-this-then-that that should never touch a language model. Around 10% is a genuine AI problem, and usually narrower than it looks.

Nuestra lectura es que la capa de coordinación debajo del trabajo de delegación, el lugar donde el contexto por empresa se ensambla, se comparte entre gerentes y se traslada de un evento al siguiente, es el punto de partida correcto. No con una herramienta nueva. Con una capa de contexto estructurado que capture lo que el equipo ya sabe y lo haga rendir a través de ciclos.

Our read is that the coordination layer under the delegation work, the place where per-company context gets assembled, shared across managers, and handed off between events, is the right place to start. Not with a new tool. With a structured context layer that captures what your team already knows and makes it compound across cycles.

Correlation One brought Eduba in to run AI enablement at Pacific Life and Colgate-Palmolive. 1,500+ people trained since May 2025. 6,000 to 9,000 hours saved per year across participating teams. 95% of trainees still using the tools 30 days after the workshop.

The shape of that engagement maps onto Uruguay XXI. Distributed cohort, regulated context, adoption measured past the 30-day cliff where most enablement programs fail.
